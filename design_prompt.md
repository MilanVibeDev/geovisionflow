🎨 UI/UX DESIGN PROMPT
🧠 Project Context

Design a modern SaaS website for:

AI Website Analyzer for Business
An AI-powered SEO + GEO + AIO (AI Optimization) audit platform.

The product analyzes websites and provides:

Technical SEO score

Geographic SEO insights

AI visibility score

Competitor gap analysis

AI-generated improvement roadmap

Target audience:

Startup founders

Marketing agencies

SEO professionals

Small business owners

The design should feel like a mix of:

Linear (clean & minimal)

Notion (structured & calm)

Stripe (modern SaaS polish)

🎨 Visual Style Guidelines
🌞 Theme

Light theme (primary)

Soft white background (#FAFBFF or similar)

Subtle gradient backgrounds

Very clean spacing

Premium SaaS feel

🌈 Color Palette (Fun but Professional)

Primary:

Electric Blue (#4F46E5 or similar)

Soft Purple (#7C3AED)

Accent:

Neon Mint (#2DD4BF)

Soft Coral (#FB7185)

Background:

Very light gray / off-white

Subtle gradient overlays

Use color for:

Scores

Buttons

Highlights

Hover states

Icons

Avoid dark heavy colors.

✨ Motion & Interactions

Add:

🔹 Hover Effects

Buttons lift slightly on hover

Subtle glow on primary CTA

Cards elevate with soft shadow

Icons slightly scale

🔹 Scroll Animations

Fade-up content sections

Staggered animation for feature cards

Parallax subtle background shapes

Smooth progress bar animation when showing scores

🔹 Microinteractions

Animated score counters

Animated SEO score gauge

Hover tooltips explaining metrics

Smooth tab transitions

Keep animations smooth, subtle, premium.
