# GeoVisionFlow - AI Visibility & Geo SEO Intelligence Platform

## 🌟 Overview
**GeoVisionFlow** (internally known as **SEO Calc**) is a state-of-the-art SEO and AI Visibility analysis platform. Unlike traditional SEO tools that focus solely on Google's ranking factors, GeoVisionFlow bridges the gap between traditional search and the new era of **Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)** and **AI Input Optimization (AIO)**.

The platform helps businesses understand how both humans and AI models (like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity) perceive their digital presence, providing actionable insights to dominate in a world where AI is the new search interface.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. Technical SEO Intelligence
*   **Deep On-Page Audit**: Analyzes meta titles, descriptions, H1-H6 structures, and image alt text.
*   **Compliance Checks**: Validates mobile viewport tags, canonical links, and robots.txt.
*   **Content Analysis**: Evaluates word count and structural depth to ensure content meets search engine standards.
*   **Page Speed Monitoring**: Integrates with Google Lighthouse to provide real-time mobile performance scores.

### 2. AIO (AI Input Optimization)
*   **LLM-Readability Score**: Measures how easily AI models can parse and extract content from your pages.
*   **AI Snippet Probability**: Estimates the likelihood of your content being featured in AI-generated answers.
*   **Topic Association**: Identifies the key concepts that AI models associate with your brand.
*   **Brand Sentiment Analysis**: Evaluates the tone and authority of your brand from an AI's perspective.

### 3. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)
*   **Local Intent Alignment**: Checks if your content provides the necessary signals for AI assistants to recommend you for local queries.
*   **Entity Recognition**: Analyzes how clearly your brand, location, and services are defined for AI crawlers.
*   **Geographic Keyword Gaps**: Suggests local-specific keywords based on target regions.

### 4. AI-Generated Strategic Roadmap
*   **Priority-Based Tasks**: Delivers a step-by-step improvement plan categorized by impact (High/Medium/Low).
*   **Contextual Suggestions**: Provides AI-written meta tags and header improvements tailored to your target keywords.
*   **Discipline Tagging**: Every task is tagged by whether it improves Technical SEO, AIO, or GEO.

### 5. Premium User Experience
*   **Dynamic Dashboard**: A modern, interactive interface featuring Framer Motion animations and real-time data visualization.
*   **Smart Caching**: Implements a robust caching layer via Supabase to ensure lightning-fast subsequent audits.
*   **Unique UI Components**: Features custom "Morph Loader" animations and particle background effects for a high-end feel.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

### Frontend
*   **Core**: [React](https://reactjs.org/) + [Vite](https://vitejs.dev/)
*   **Styling**: Vanilla CSS with modern flex/grid layouts and CSS variables for theming.
*   **Animations**: [Framer Motion](https://www.framer.com/motion/)
*   **Icons**: [Lucide React](https://lucide.dev/)
*   **Components**: Custom-built UI library including shadcn-inspired patterns.

### Backend
*   **Runtime**: [Node.js](https://nodejs.org/)
*   **Framework**: [Express.js](https://expressjs.com/)
*   **Database/Caching**: [Supabase](https://supabase.com/) (PostgreSQL)
*   **Web Scraping**: Custom Node.js crawling engine using `axios` and `cheerio`.

### AI & APIs
*   **Intelligence**: [Google Gemini API](https://ai.google.dev/) / [OpenRouter](https://openrouter.ai/) for high-level semantic analysis.
*   **Performance**: Google PageSpeed Insights API.

## 📈 Development Process

1.  **Conceptualization**: Defined the "AIO" and "GEO" scoring metrics to differentiate the tool from legacy SEO platforms.
2.  **Architecture Design**: Built a decoupled MERN-like architecture with a focus on high-concurrency scraping and AI processing.
3.  **Prompt Engineering**: Developed sophisticated AI prompts to ensure scoring rationale and improvement tasks are accurate and professional.
4.  **UI/UX Iteration**: Focused on a "Data-at-a-Glance" philosophy, ensuring complex AI metrics are represented through intuitive circular progress bars and priority lists.
5.  **Performance Optimization**: Engineered a backend queue and caching system to handle intensive scraping tasks efficiently.
